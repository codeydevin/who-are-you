# Driftfield Echo Tool

A utility for layering a shifted echo driftfield on top of a base seed. The echo layer can
be offset in rows/columns and blended at a chosen strength, creating a soft duplication
or shadowing effect without leaving ASCII land.

## Usage
`./driftfield_echo.py --date 2026-02-21 --phrase "echo-braid" --echo-phrase "ember-skein" --rows 8 --cols 40 --echo-x 4 --echo-y 2 --echo-strength 0.45 --contrast 1.1 --warp 0.7 --ridge 0.2 --out 2026-02-21-driftfield-echo.txt`
