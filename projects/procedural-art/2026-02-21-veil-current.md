# Veil Current (Driftfield Study)

A mid-density driftfield tuned for a calm front with a slight eastward tilt. This study leans on
soft warp and light ridge blending to keep the texture cohesive while still showing a few sharp
bands.

Parameters
- Date: 2026-02-21
- Seed phrase: veil-current
- Grid: 22x66
- Octaves: 4 (persistence 0.6, lacunarity 2.1)
- Smooth passes: 2
- Contrast: 1.15
- Warp: 1.4
- Ridge: 0.25
- Bias: -0.02
- Tilt X: 0.05
- Tilt Y: -0.03
